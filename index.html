Welcome to Moon Shadow Ranch!

Thank you for visiting us! We are very excited to have you here!

Moon Shadow Ranch is a small farm in a sunny southern California town called Rainbow. We have numerous animals and are very horticulturally active.

While the internet only allows us to offer a glimpse into who we are and what we do, we encourage you to take this opportunity to browse around. You will find information about us, our horses, and our dairy goats. There are plenty of pictures in our gallery to check out too. If you're interested in learning more, contact us - we'd be happy to chat with you!
