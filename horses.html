Ezis Arabians

A much loved part of our farm is Ezis Arabians, a small Arabian horse breeding operation. Ezis Arabians started with the gorgeous and gentle Stella Luna VS (Baskemo x Eters Windsong). This tall mare brought with her the undeniable beauty and breathtaking motion that she has passed on to her progeny. Our attention soon turned towards complimenting Stella's refinement with the bone density and mass necessary for endurance purposes.

After much research, we finally found the right breeding partner: Al-Marah Arabians. With Al-Marah's focus on reigning and cutting, we found their breeding objectives to meet our needs. Numerous discussions ensued along with several trips to Tucson, Arizona. It was there that we found Al-Marah Garcon (Canadian Beau x AM Tis Beverlie). A year passed and Stella blessed us with his baby, a filly whose blood carries generations of motion that most people equate to floating on air. With her mother's beauty and disposition, MS Claire De Lune will capture your heart before a moment's notice.

A little bonus resulting from our relationship with Al-Marah was the discovery of an excellent brood mare that embodied what we sought. This horse, AM Shara Joke (Deep Freeze x Finale Seranade), soon joined us at Moon Shadow Ranch and foaled MS Debussy (Seagfreed + x AM Shara Joke), who was an outstandingly solid colt and has become a strong, sound, and handsome stallion. Much to our pleasure, his temperament is actually milder than many geldings and he is an absolute joy to work with.

The most recent additions to the herd include Debussy's brother, MS Shara Dream by AM Power Raid, and sister, MS Shara Toy by IMA Tonka Toy. We also had tremendous success with our first in-house breeding resulting in a delightful colt named MS Debussy's Prelude (MS Debussy x Stella Luna VS), who vibrantly bears his mother's grace and his father's sturdy legs.
